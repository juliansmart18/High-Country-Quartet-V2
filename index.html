Western North Carolina's Premier String Ensemble

Unique, vibrant renditions of popular hits for unforgettable weddings and events.

Our identity is rooted in the mountains of North Carolina.

Our passion is crafting the ideal musical backdrop for events in the High Country and beyond.

Our members are active symphonic musicians, regularly performing with orchestras across North Carolina and Tennessee.

Although our training is rooted in classical music, we came together with a shared vision of creating fresh and vibrant string interpretations of popular music across all genres.

We take pride in our versatility and ability to provide high quality music for any occasion.

"I kept getting comments about how good the music was at my wedding!"

We have a large library of music that is continuously growing.

We have hundreds of hand-picked string quartet arrangements of popular music across various genres.

Some of our genres of music include classical, pop, R&B, jazz, soundtracks, folk, country, and holiday music. See some of our setlists below!
